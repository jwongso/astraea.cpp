Tips for a better answer
  • Describe what happened — what the landlord is claiming or doing, and when it started.
  • Include key amounts — bond total, cleaning costs, rent owed, or repair quotes.
  • Mention what you have already done — paid for a clean, sent a written notice, took photos, etc.
  • Say what you need — explain my rights, what are my next steps, or draft a letter / email.

Example: “My landlord is withholding my $2,000 bond claiming carpet damage. I paid $160 for a professional clean and the carpet was already 8 years old when I moved in. What are my rights and can you draft a letter disputing this?”
